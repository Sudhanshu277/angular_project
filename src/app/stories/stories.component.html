Top Dropout Entreprenures in india



Ankit Oberoi

Ankit Oberoi is co-founder of Adpushup, which provides optimization of ads. He dropped out from College in the first semester, in his words, " You don’t have to be at the college to learn something – Internet can teach you better."

Varun Shoor

A self-taught programmer at 13 and web designer, he took a natural interest in the development of web applications. He started the company in 2001 when he was just 17 with its first office in Jalandhar after dropping out of college.

Ritesh Agarwal

Ritesh Agarwal is the man behind the ‘Ola’ for rooms, OYO Rooms. The startup is a network of technology – enabled budget hotels. This Gurgaon-based company, was founded by him in 2012.A college dropout who founded Oravel when he was 18 received its share of fundings and accolades and later he rebranded it to OYO Rooms.
